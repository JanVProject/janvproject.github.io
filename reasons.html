Reasons why YOU should become a foster parent

How can you help solving these issues bothering thousands of Croatian children?

What does it mean to foster a child?

In definition, fostering a child is taking care of a child for a limited time, without being the child's legal parents.

What it actually means is much more important:

Fostering a child means opening your heart to an often neglected and abused child and by offering your home to them, being a crucial part in their growing up. Even though they may not be your child, and they might only stay for a limtied period, the love foster parents feel for their foster children is irreplacable in their growing up and development.

Children in Croatian foster homes need you

Thousands of Croatian children in foster homes are waiting for someone to englighten their childhoods. This "someone" could be just you.

You care about children and are ready to help them

Many children within the foster care system, especially in foster homes have often been abused, neglected, forgotten, and have and continue to face challenges. Fostering children is a powerful way in lifting up children and showing them a path to a better life and future.

You want to lower the pressure in foster homes

By fostering children at their youngest ages, parents are actually contributing to releasing pressure from foster homes and social care workers. By lowering the number of children in foster homes, those that must stay in foster homes will have a much more pleasent stay.

You are willing to use your life skills, love and knowledge to a worthwile cause

Children are impressionable beings and require parents who are ready to share all love, knowledge and experiences with them.

You have personally faced many different challenges in life and are ready to help impressionable children in overcoming their fears, traumas and sadnesses

Sharing your personal experiences with children at their youngest, you can be the first in helping them more easily pass through life's difficult times they have experienced already.

You enjoy a challenge and are ready to expericence

Each child is very different and has different needs and/or issues. You are ready to cater to the needs of a child because by doing this you will be a crucial part in their life and growing up.

You have enough space for older children and sibling groups and want to stop the breaking of the sibling bond

The bond between siblings is one of the longest and strongest relationships a person can have in their life. Croatia finds a very difficult time in finding enough space for siblings, which causes them to often be seperated. For children who have already experienced pain being taken away from parents, the separation from siblings is heart-breaking. By fostering children you will help protect those irreplacable bonds.

You want to impact your community

Foster parenting goes much further than helping an individual child. Foster parents help fight the problems of homelessness, substance abuse, mental health issues, domestic abuse poverty and others. While caring for foster children, foster parents give the birth parents of the child the chance to receive the necessary help to overcome problems that may have led to the removal of the children from their home. Foster parents can also be role models to birth parents on better ways of parenting the children.

You will make a mark on the child you foster, forever. The love and experience you give them will shape them as a person forever.
